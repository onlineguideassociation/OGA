📘 ONLINE GUIDE ASSOCIATION
Official Proposal Document

Empowering Cambodian Tour Guides Through Digital Infrastructure

🌍 1️⃣ Executive Summary

The Online Guide Association (OGA) is a Cambodia-based tourism initiative designed to:

Digitize local tour guides

Improve income stability

Increase global visibility

Provide fundraising & training support

Preserve Khmer cultural heritage

Launching first in:

Siem Reap

Phnom Penh

Around Angkor Wat

OGA integrates:

Guide Profiles

Booking System

CRM

AI Support Tools

Fundraising for Tour Guides

Digital Education

🏛 2️⃣ Background & Problem Statement
4

Cambodian tour guides face:

Seasonal income instability

Limited digital marketing skills

No centralized professional platform

High dependency on third-party agencies

Limited access to funding or training

Many talented guides working around Angkor lack:

Professional websites

International exposure

Sustainable income structure

🎯 3️⃣ Vision & Mission
🌟 Vision

To become the digital infrastructure for tourism professionals in Cambodia and Southeast Asia.

🎯 Mission

Digitize 1,000 Cambodian tour guides

Increase average guide income by 30–50%

Provide access to education & fundraising

Promote responsible tourism

🛠 4️⃣ Proposed Solution
A. Digital Guide Platform (OnlineGuide.io)

Each guide receives:

Professional profile page

Online booking system

Payment integration

AI marketing assistant

Review management

B. GuideFund (Fundraising Program)

Allows guides to raise funds for:

Training & language improvement

License certification

Equipment upgrade

Community tourism projects

C. Education & Digital Training

Programs include:

English for Tourism

Digital Marketing

Social Media Branding

Cultural Heritage Storytelling

Ethical Tourism Practices

💼 5️⃣ Target Beneficiaries

Licensed tour guides

Young tourism graduates

Women guides

Rural community tourism leaders

Drivers expanding into guiding

💰 6️⃣ Revenue & Sustainability Model

OGA will sustain operations through:

Training program fees
