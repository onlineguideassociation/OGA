🌏 OnlineGuide.io – CRM & Marketing Platform for Tourism

OnlineGuide.io includes a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and marketing system designed to help tourism businesses manage marketing, sales, bookings, and customer service in one platform.

Inspired by platforms like HubSpot, OnlineGuide.io provides tools that help tour operators, hotels, travel agencies, and tourism brands grow their customer base and manage relationships with travelers.

🔎 What the OnlineGuide.io CRM Is

OnlineGuide.io offers an all-in-one tourism business platform that helps companies:

• attract travelers
• convert visitors into bookings
• manage reservations and leads
• support customers after their trip

It combines marketing, booking management, and CRM tools into a single connected system.
