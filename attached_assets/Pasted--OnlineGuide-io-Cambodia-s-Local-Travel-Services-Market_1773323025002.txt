🌏 OnlineGuide.io – Cambodia’s Local Travel & Services Marketplace

OnlineGuide.io is a consumer-to-consumer (C2C) online marketplace for travel, tours, and local services in Cambodia. It connects travelers, local guides, tour operators, and businesses so they can buy, sell, and promote tourism-related experiences and services.

Inspired by platforms like Khmer24, OnlineGuide.io focuses on tourism and travel rather than general classifieds.

🔎 What OnlineGuide.io Does

OnlineGuide.io allows users to:

Post listings for tours, travel services, or local experiences

Browse travel experiences by category or location

Book tours or hire local services directly

Contact service providers safely through the platform

This enables travelers to access authentic local experiences while providing income opportunities for guides, photographers, and travel professionals.

📦 What You Can Find on OnlineGuide.io

Popular categories include:

🧭 Tours & Activities – Sightseeing, adventure, cultural tours
🏨 Hotels & Accommodations – Boutique hotels, guesthouses, homestays
🎨 Local Experiences & Creatives – Photography tours, art workshops, cultural performances
🛶 Adventure & Outdoor Activities – Kayaking, trekking, cycling tours
📊 Professional Travel Services – Travel planning, itinerary creation, guides

Users can also find short-term rentals, transport services, and travel-related products.

🧠 How OnlineGuide.io Works
1️⃣ Post Your Listing

Local guides, operators, and travel professionals create listings with:

Service description

Pricing or packages

Photos and itineraries

Available dates

2️⃣ Browse & Book

Travelers search by category, destination, or dates, review listings, and book experiences directly through the platform.

3️⃣ Payment & Communication

OnlineGuide.io facilitates:

Secure online payments

Booking confirmations

Messaging between traveler and provider

Safe and transparent transactions

📊 Market Position

OnlineGuide.io is focused on Cambodia, serving travelers and local service providers in major cities like Phnom Penh, Siem Reap, and Sihanoukville.

It builds on the success of local marketplaces like Khmer24 but specializes in tourism and travel services, helping Cambodia’s tourism industry thrive while connecting travelers to authentic experiences.

🌍 Local Focus

Unlike global marketplaces like Lazada or Amazon, OnlineGuide.io emphasizes:

✔ Authentic local tours and experiences
✔ Listings in Khmer and English
✔ Direct booking of travel services
✔ Community-driven local commerce for tourism

💡 Why OnlineGuide.io Matters

OnlineGuide.io empowers local travel professionals and offers travelers:

Access to authentic cultural and adventure experiences

Safe, secure booking and communication

Opportunities for locals to earn income directly from travelers

A marketplace focused entirely on Cambodian tourism and services
