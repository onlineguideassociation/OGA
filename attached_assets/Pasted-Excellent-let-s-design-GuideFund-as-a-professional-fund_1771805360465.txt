💰 GuideFund – Project Fundraising Flow
1️⃣ STEP 1: Guide Creates a Project

Guide submits:

• Project Title
• Category (Content Creator / Photographer / Promotion / Equipment / Community)
• Funding Goal ($500 – $10,000+)
• Timeline (30 / 60 / 90 days)
• Budget Breakdown
• Photos & Videos
• Expected Impact

Example:

🎥 “Angkor Sunrise Documentary Series”
Goal: $2,000
Use: Camera upgrade + Editing + Promotion

2️⃣ STEP 2: Platform Review & Approval

Online Guide Association verifies:

✔ Real licensed guide
✔ Clear purpose
✔ Realistic budget
✔ Transparent use of funds

Approved → Project goes LIVE.

3️⃣ STEP 3: Project Page Structure

Each project has a mini landing page:
