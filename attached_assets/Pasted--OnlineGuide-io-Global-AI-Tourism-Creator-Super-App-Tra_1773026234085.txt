🌏 OnlineGuide.io — Global AI Tourism & Creator Super-App + Travel Experiences Marketplace

Headquarters: 🇰🇭 Cambodia (launching from Angkor Wat, Siem Reap, Phnom Penh)
Platform Type: AI-powered tourism ecosystem, creator tools, marketplace, and freelance platform

Purpose: Connect travelers, guides, creators, businesses, and investors in a single intelligent ecosystem that unifies travel planning, content creation, bookings, commerce, and investment workflows.

1️⃣ Platform Vision & Core Values

Mission: Become the AI Operating System for Global Tourism — replacing fragmented apps with a unified, intelligent ecosystem.

Core Values:

Accessibility: Multilingual AI support (English, Khmer, Chinese, Korean, Japanese, French, Hindi, Bahasa, Tagalog, Lao)

Sustainability: Protects communities, culture, and environment

Personalization: AI recommends experiences, tours, products, and investments

Integration: Connects travel, commerce, finance, media, and startups

2️⃣ Ecosystem Modules & Features
Module	Key Functions
AI Travel OS	Smart itineraries, GPS voice tours, multilingual narration, tourism trend prediction
Knowledge Graph	Connects tourism, media, commerce, food, education, real estate, agriculture; powers AI recommendations
Marketplace & Commerce	Product marketplaces, affiliate programs, dropshipping, ROI analytics
Financial AI Assistant	Personal finance, startup planning, enterprise investment tools
Media Ecosystem	AI photo/video editing, creator monetization, travel storytelling
Sustainability Mapping	Eco-tourism, agro-tourism, community tourism projects
Startup Investment Networks	Investor discovery, fundraising CRM, pitch analytics, warm introductions
Global Travel Experiences Marketplace	Tours, activities, excursions, attraction tickets, operator booking management
3️⃣ Technology & Architecture

Frontend: React, Tailwind CSS, React Native

Backend: Node.js + Express

Database: MongoDB & Firebase

Graph Visualization: React Flow & D3.js

Deployment: Replit primary, optional AWS/Google Cloud scaling

🔹 How the Pieces Work Together

Travelers access tours, activities, and AI-guided itineraries.

Knowledge Graph connects all modules — providing insights, personalization, and recommendations.

AI Layers enhance travel planning, media intelligence, business analytics, and marketing automation.

Media & Financial AI support creators and guides with storytelling, monetization, budgeting, and investment insights.

Community, Marketplace & IDE allow collaboration, freelance work, content creation, and e-commerce.

Integrated Ecosystem ensures all users — travelers, guides, creators, and investors — interact within one unified AI-powered platform.
